# ZYNVARO — 2-Minute Demo Voiceover Script (Final)

> **How to use this file:**
> 1. Convert this to audio (ElevenLabs, NotebookLM, or record yourself)
> 2. Play the audio in your earphones
> 3. Open `https://devtrails-zynvaro.onrender.com/app`
> 4. Screen-record while following the `→` instructions
> 5. `[PAUSE]` = hold the screen still, let the visual breathe
> 6. `[BEAT]` = half-second pause between sentences

## RECORDING TIPS

1. **The silence at 1:10–1:13 is everything.** When the WhatsApp modal slides up, STOP talking. Let the green notification fill the screen. Every other team will be talking over their demo. Your silence will make judges lean forward. This is your mic-drop moment.

2. **Say "eight hundred and twelve" not "812".** Spoken numbers are more impactful than digits. Same for "two hundred tree" not "200-tree".

3. **Slow down at the close.** The last 8 seconds should feel like a movie ending. Drop your voice slightly. Leave space between "Zynvaro" and the tagline.

4. **The tooltip hover (1:47)** is strategic — it shows judges that YOU know the difference between demo and production, without saying "this is a demo." It's a subtle flex.

5. **Don't show registration.** It's boring. Starting from login and immediately showing the dashboard is more impactful. Judges know registration exists — they don't need to watch you type a name.

6. **Record at 1x speed.** Never speed up the video. Judges want to see real performance. If the app loads fast, that's a flex. If you speed it up, they'll think you're hiding lag.
