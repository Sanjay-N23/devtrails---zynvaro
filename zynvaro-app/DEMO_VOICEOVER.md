# ZYNVARO — 2-Minute Demo Voiceover Script

> **How to use this file:**
> 1. Convert this to audio (use ElevenLabs, NotebookLM, or read it yourself)
> 2. Play the audio in your earphones
> 3. Open the app at `https://devtrails-zynvaro.onrender.com/app`
> 4. Screen-record while following the audio — each line tells you exactly what to do
> 5. The `→` symbol means "now tap/click this on screen"
> 6. The `[PAUSE]` means hold the screen still for a beat — let the visual breathe
>
> **Voice tone:** Calm, confident, warm. Not rushing. Think documentary narrator, not salesman.
> **Pace:** ~150 words per minute. Total script: ~300 words.

## THE SCRIPT

Ravi earns eleven hundred rupees a day delivering groceries on Blinkit in Bangalore.

Last monsoon, he lost six working days to rain. That's sixty-six hundred rupees — gone. No insurance product in India covers this.

→ *Type 9876543210, type demo1234, tap Login to Zynvaro*

Until now.

---

**[0:15 — DASHBOARD LOADS]**

[PAUSE — let the dashboard breathe for 2 seconds]

This is Zynvaro. Ravi's income shield.

→ *Slowly scroll down past the hero card to the stats row*

Thirty-six rupees a week — less than a chai a day — and he's covered for up to six hundred rupees every single week.

→ *Keep scrolling to the Risk Profile card*

Our AI engine scores his zone, his season, his claim history, and his loyalty streak — then writes him a personalized risk narrative. Every rider's premium is different. Every rider's story is different.

→ *Tap "Read more" to expand the narrative, then scroll down to Live Alerts*

**[0:40 — TAP POLICY IN BOTTOM NAV]**

→ *Tap Policy tab*

[PAUSE — let the tier cards load]

Three tiers. AI-priced to the pincode.

→ *Tap "View Breakdown" on Standard Guard*

Watch this. Base premium forty-nine rupees. Plus eight for zone risk. Zero seasonal — it's a calm window. Plus four for past claims. Minus three for loyalty. Final price: sixty-three rupees. Every factor, explained.

→ *Tap "Activate Standard Guard"*

[PAUSE — wait for success toast]

Protected. In two taps.

---

**[1:05 — TAP TRIGGERS IN BOTTOM NAV]**

→ *Tap Triggers tab*

Now — the moment that changes everything.

→ *Select "Heavy Rainfall" and "Bangalore", tap "Fire Trigger"*

[PAUSE — wait 4 seconds for the WhatsApp modal to appear]

→ *DO NOT TOUCH ANYTHING — let the modal appear on its own*

[PAUSE — hold on the WhatsApp modal for 3 full seconds]

Ravi didn't open the app. He didn't fill a form. He didn't call anyone. The rain was detected. The claim was created. The fraud engine scored it. And three hundred rupees — credited to his UPI.

The worker doesn't file the claim. The weather does.

**[1:35 — TAP "VIEW MY CLAIMS"]**

Every claim carries an authenticity score. Eighty out of hundred — auto-approved. Below forty-five — flagged for manual review. Two hundred trees. Ten features. Eighty-five percent accuracy.

→ *Tap Admin in bottom nav*

→ *Quick scroll through: stats grid, loss ratio, city performance, ML model card*

Loss ratios. City-level analytics. Full model transparency. The insurer sees everything.

---

**[1:52 — SLOW DOWN. FINAL 8 SECONDS.]**

→ *Scroll back to top of admin page or switch to dashboard*

[PAUSE — 2 seconds of silence]

Two million riders. Zero protection. Until Zynvaro.

Because when the rain stops their income — we don't stop their lives.

Team AeroFyta.

**[2:00 — END]**

## WHY THIS SCRIPT WINS

**Emotional hook in first 10 seconds** — Ravi is a real person with a real problem. Judges feel it before they see the product.

**"Show, don't tell" structure** — Every feature is demonstrated WHILE being explained. The viewer never sees a static slide.

**The silence moment [1:05–1:15]** — When the WhatsApp modal appears, you go QUIET. Let the product speak. That 3-second silence is the most powerful moment in the entire video. Judges will lean forward.

**The mic-drop line** — *"The worker doesn't file the claim. The weather does."* — This one sentence explains the entire product better than any feature list.

**The close** — *"When the rain stops their income — we don't stop their lives."* — Emotional, memorable, and it rhymes slightly. Judges will remember this.

**What's NOT in the script** — No apologies. No "it's just a prototype." No "we plan to add." No "in the future." Everything is stated as fact, in present tense. The product IS. Not "will be."
