⚡ Parametric Triggers

6 Measurable Triggers That Fire Payouts

Every trigger is objectively measurable via public APIs, correlated with income loss, and independently verifiable.

🌧️

Heavy Rainfall

≥ 64.5 mm / 24hr

IMD "Heavy Rain" threshold. Dual-source: OpenWeatherMap + IMD API. Causes 40-90% income loss.

🌊

Extreme Flooding

≥ 204.5 mm / 24hr

IMD "Extremely Heavy" or NDMA Red Alert. SACHET + GDACS dual-source. 70-100% earnings loss.

🔥

Severe Heatwave

≥ 45°C for 2+ days

IMD heatwave criteria. OpenWeatherMap + IMD Bulletins. 20-40% weekly loss. Delhi: 10-25 days/year.

🏭

Hazardous AQI

AQI ≥ 401 for 24hrs

WAQI + CPCB dual-source. "Severe" classification. 15-30% weekly loss. Delhi: 30-50 days/winter.

☁️

Platform Outage

HTTP 503 > 15 min

Synthetic monitoring + Downdetector. ~100% income loss. Cloudflare Dec 2025 outage precedent.

🚨

Civil Disruption

Section 144 ≥ 4 hrs

GDELT + NewsAPI + gazette feeds. Section 144/curfew. 60-80% daily income loss.

🛡️ Anti-Spoofing

4-Layer Fraud Detection Pipeline

GPS is only 10% of our authenticity score. We have 6 other signals spoofers can't fake.

1

Telemetry Validation

Isolation Forest cross-references GPS × Wi-Fi BSSID × Cell Tower × IP. Sub-second detection.

2

Behavioral Anomaly

Autoencoder + LSTM analyzes accelerometer/gyroscope. Home flatline ≠ stranded-in-rain motion.

3

Network Collusion

GNN + Louvain detects syndicate clusters via shared IPs, device IDs, bank accounts.

4

Cross-Reference

XGBoost validates claim against IMD + CPCB + GDACS + TomTom. Multi-source consensus.
